About This Project

PuzzleSnap AI

"Capture. Shuffle. Solve." — A privacy-first AI photo puzzle game.

What is it?

PuzzleSnap AI is an interactive web experience that blends computer vision, real-time hand gesture recognition, live camera capture, and classic sliding-puzzle gameplay into a single seamless session. Point your webcam at yourself, flash a thumbs-up, and your photo becomes the puzzle.

How the privacy model works

Most "AI camera" apps send your video stream to a remote server for processing. PuzzleSnap AI does not. Gesture detection runs entirely inside your browser as a WebAssembly module (MediaPipe Tasks Vision). The raw camera feed never touches the network. Only the single JPEG frame at the moment of capture is transmitted — and even that is processed in server RAM, re-encoded, sent back, and immediately discarded. Nothing reaches a database, filesystem, or cloud service.

  • Live camera stream: stays on your device
  • Captured frame: processed in RAM, returned, then deleted
  • Database: anonymous scores only — no images, no names
  • sessionStorage: automatically cleared when the tab closes or the puzzle is solved
  • Cloud storage: none
  • Third-party analytics: none
  • Ads: none

Puzzle engine

Once a photo is captured and enhanced, slicing happens client-side on a hidden <canvas>. Each piece is drawn by reading a sub-rectangle of the source image onto its own canvas element, which is then placed as a positioned <div> on the board. Drag-and-drop is handled through the Pointer Events API (single code path for mouse and touch) with a magnetic snap zone at 28 % of piece size. All of this runs at 60 fps with no server communication.

Scoring formula

Score = base × 0.2 + time_bonus + moves_bonus − hint_penalty, where the base ranges from 1 000 (Easy) to 5 500 (Expert). Time and move bonuses are capped at 2× par, so a perfect run roughly doubles the base score. Each hint subtracts 5 % of base. The same formula is used in the browser for the live estimate and on the server for the authoritative record.
